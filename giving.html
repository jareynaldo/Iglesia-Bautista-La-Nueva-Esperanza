Ways To Give

Here are ways you can give to the ministry and life of our church:

In-Person Item2 Item 3

In-Person

Offering plates will be passed during our weekly congregational worship service.

Online

Click the Give button above and you will need to enter the amount you wish to give and an email address. The next page will allow you to choose whether to give using your checking/savings account or a credit/debit card. Please be aware that there is a 3% fee for the church, however, you will receive full credit for your donation. You have the option to choose how often you would like the donation to be made. It can be a one-time gift or a recurring contribution. You will be sent a receipt by email.

Check

If you currently donate by check, you can mail your check to the church. We can also collect it every sunday during the In-Person serice.

Thank you for supporting our church!

If you have any questions about giving options, please call the church office at (305) 434-6332.
